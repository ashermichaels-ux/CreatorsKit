Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Overview

CreatorKit ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website at ashermichaels-ux.github.io/CreatorsKit. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This site is free to use and requires no account or login.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data directly. Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account
  • We do not collect your name, email, or any identifying information
  • Text you type into our tools is sent directly to Gemini (Google) — we never see or store it
  • We do not use cookies of our own

Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services which may collect data independently:

  • Google AdSense — displays ads and may use cookies to show relevant ads. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini — when you click a tool button, your input and a prompt are sent to Gemini's website in a new tab. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's servers. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Affiliate links — links to Jasper AI, Canva, Epidemic Sound, and Notion may use cookies to track referrals. See each company's respective privacy policy.

Cookies

CreatorKit itself only stores one item in your browser's local storage: your dark/light mode preference. This never leaves your device and is not shared with anyone.

Third-party services (Google AdSense, affiliate partners) may set their own cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings.

Affiliate Links

Some links on this site are affiliate links. This means we may earn a small commission if you click through and make a purchase — at no extra cost to you. We only recommend tools we believe are genuinely useful for content creators.

Children's Privacy

CreatorKit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us via the GitHub repository at github.com/ashermichaels-ux/CreatorsKit.
